Goodbye Jackie

I designed the obituary for Aunt Jackie's passing. She was not my real aunt; it was what everyone called her. Aunt Jackie used to drop her nephew, my best friend, and me off to school. She had a funny personality and was a caring person. She was loved by many. Rest in peace, Aunt Jackie, we miss you.

Programs used:

  • InDesign

Tokyo Punk

This is me experimenting with typography and textures. I call it Tokyo Punk. Tokyo is spray-painted white on black leather with a splash of red underlining the word. This piece gives off the vibe that whoever spray-painted Tokyo over this piece of leather didn't care.

Programs used:

  • Photoshop
